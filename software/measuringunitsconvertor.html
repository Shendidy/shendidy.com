Measuring Units Convertor 1.0.0.7

About:

This program converts between measuring units of Lengths, Areas, Volume, pressure and Speed.

It's very easy to use and doesn't take time to downlaod or install (558kb)






How to:



From the top tabs, choose Length, Area, Volume, Weight or Pressure to change any quantity you have from one measuring unit to the other.


From the dropdown lists, "From" and "To", choose the units you want to convert between. Type in the quantity you want to convert, then click "Calculate", that simple. then if you wish, click the "Copy" button to copy the claculated value into your clipboard and paste it into another document, Excel, Word or anywhere else.


Below is a list of the units this program supports:


Length:

- Kilometer, Meter, Decimeter, Centimeter, Millimeter, Inch, Foot, Yard, Mile, Nautical mile.


Area:

- Squared Kilometers, Squared Meters, Squared Decimeters, Squared Centimeters, Squared Millimeters, Squared Inch, Squared Foot, Squared Yard, Squared Miles, Squared Nautical Miles, Acres, Hectares, Feddans.


Volume:

Cubic Kilometers, Cubic Meters, Cubic Decimeters, Cubic Centimeters, Cubic Millimeters, Cubic Inch, Cubic Foot, Cubic Yard, Cubic Miles, Cubic Nautical Miles, Litres, Oil Barrel, Imperial Gallons, US Gallons.


Weight:

- Tonnes, KiloGrams, Grams, KiloNewtons, Newtons, Pounds, Stones.


Pressure:

- Pascal, KiloPascal, Bar, At, Atm, PSI, MilliBar, mmHg, N/mm2, N/cm2, N/dm2, N/m2, N/in2, N/ft2, N/Yrd2, KN/mm2, KN/cm2, KN/dm2, KN/m2, KN/in2, KN/ft2, KN/Yrd2, g/mm2, g/cm2, g/dm2, g/m2, g/in2, g/ft2, g/Yrd2, Kg/mm2, Kg/cm2, Kg/dm2, Kg/m2, Kg/in2, Kg/ft2, Kg/Yrd2, Ton/mm2, Ton/cm2, Ton/dm2, Ton/m2, Ton/in2, Ton/ft2, Ton/Yrd2, lb/mm2, lb/cm2, lb/dm2, lb/m2, lb/in2 (PSI), lb/ft2, lb/Yrd2
